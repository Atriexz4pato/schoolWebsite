Co-curricum in Enlight

In Enlight, co-curricular activities complement the academic curriculum and provide students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and foster personal growth outside of traditional classroom learning. These activities are usually organized and supervised by teachers or staff members and can include a wide range of options to cater to different interests and talents among students.

BasketBall

The institution also features basketball as a prominent athletic activity. The gymnasium serves as a lively center for these hardwood endeavors, where students unite each season to showcase their on-court abilities. These basketball performances, complementing the eagerly anticipated aquatic spectacles in the natatorium, consistently garner enthusiastic appreciation from the school community.

swimming

The school's natatorium is a center of aquatic vitality. Throughout the year, the students unite to refine their water-based abilities, exhibiting their swimming, diving, and rescue maneuvers. From high-speed contests to choreographed displays, the aquatic spectacles are consistently met with enthusiastic anticipation from the school populace.

Drama

The high school drama club is a hub of creative energy. Each semester, the students come together to put on a production, showcasing their acting, directing, and technical skills. From classic plays to modern adaptations, the performances are always eagerly anticipated by the school community.
